Understanding the Conference

What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an authentic, dynamic simulation of the UN General Assembly, UN Security Council, or other multilateral bodies. It introduces students to the high-stakes world of international diplomacy and negotiation.

Delegates step into the shoes of ambassadors to draft resolutions, plot strategy, negotiate with supporters and adversaries, and resolve conflicts.

Our Vision

GD Goenka Public School, Sector 10A, Gurugram stands as a beacon of educational excellence, committed to nurturing global citizens with critical thinking and compassion.

The GD Goenka MUN is our flagship intra-school event. It serves as a crucible for young minds to discuss pressing global issues, build unbreakable confidence, and master the art of consensus building in a divided world.

Why Participate? Core Skills

Public Speaking

Conquer stage fright and articulate complex thoughts clearly to an engaged audience.

Critical Thinking

Analyze geopolitical situations quickly, draft sound policies, and think on your feet.

Diplomacy & Networking

Master the subtle art of negotiation while building a strong network with brilliant peers.

Campus Life

About the GD Goenka Campus

The School is situated on a vast 6 acres lush green campus, its sprawling campus, with an impressive and specially designed building, well laid – out play fields, most upto date facilities and above all, its tradition of excellence, fee structure and scholarships, give the school a unique status among its kind. Every year the school designs new paradigms that bring highly educated, creative teachers together with students to engage in learning activities that are interactive and customized to students’ interests and needs.

With dedicated conference areas, digital resources, and student-centric facilities, the campus is designed to support a memorable and engaging MUN experience.
